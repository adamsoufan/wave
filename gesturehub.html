Create Gesture Mapping

  1. Step 1: Select Macro

    Choose the macro you want to map to a hand gesture (e.g., "Save and Close").

  2. Step 2: Configure Gesture

    Define the hand gesture that will trigger the selected macro.

  3. Step 3: Test Mapping

    Test the mapping by performing the gesture while the webcam feed is active.

  4. Step 4: Finalize

    Click "Save Mapping" to finalize and store the gesture-to-macro assignment.
