Create a Keypress Macro

  1. Step 1: Define the Macro

    Specify a name and description for your macro (e.g., "Save and Close").

  2. Step 2: Record Key Sequence

    Record the keypress sequence: CTRL+S followed by ALT+F4.

  3. Step 3: Review & Edit

    Review the key sequence for accuracy and adjust if needed.

  4. Step 4: Save Macro

    Click "Save" to store your macro in the Macro Hub.
